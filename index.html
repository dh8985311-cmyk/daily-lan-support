Manage subscription & billing

Daily‑Lan subscriptions are handled securely by Apple. To change or cancel a plan:

  1. On your iPhone, open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions.
  2. Select Daily‑Lan and choose Cancel or switch plans.

Restore purchases

Reinstalled the app or switched devices? In Daily‑Lan, go to Profile > Restore Purchases to re‑activate an existing subscription.

Free trial

If your plan includes a free trial, you won’t be charged until the trial ends. Cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends to avoid charges.

Troubleshooting

  • App not syncing / loading? Check your internet connection and try again.
  • Audio / microphone issues? Ensure microphone permission is enabled in Settings > Daily‑Lan.
  • Pronunciation not scoring? Move to a quieter place and speak near the mic.
  • App crashes? Update to the latest version from the App Store and reboot your device.

FAQ

What is Daily‑Lan?

Daily‑Lan helps you practice English conversation with AI, assess pronunciation, and track vocabulary & grammar achievements—daily.

How do I change my subscription?

Open iOS Settings > Subscriptions, select Daily‑Lan, then choose a different plan or cancel.

I purchased but Premium isn’t unlocked

Tap Restore Purchases in the app. If it still fails, email us with your Apple ID (masked) and purchase date.

How do refunds work?

Apple manages refunds. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow the instructions.

Do I need an account to subscribe?

No login is required to purchase. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored anytime.
