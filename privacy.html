Daily-Lan • Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 30, 2025

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Daily-Lan (“we”, “us”, “our”). This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. If you have questions, contact us at minhhai12anqb@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

  • Identifiers & Contact: email (when you contact support), device identifiers (for app functionality and subscriptions).
  • Purchase & Subscription Status: product identifiers, transaction receipts, subscription state (via Apple In‑App Purchase and our subscription provider).
  • Usage & Diagnostics: app version, device & OS info, crash logs, limited analytics to improve stability and features.
  • User Content: optional learning inputs (e.g., prompts or recordings for pronunciation assessment) processed to provide features, not used for advertising.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the app’s core features (AI conversation, pronunciation assessment, vocabulary & grammar modules).
  • Verify and manage in‑app subscriptions and free trials.
  • Customer support and service communications.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Sharing & Service Providers

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help deliver the app, such as:

  • Apple App Store for in‑app purchases and subscription validation.
  • RevenueCat for subscription management (receipt validation, entitlement status). See their privacy practices for details.
  • Other processors (e.g., crash reporting or analytics) when enabled to improve reliability and performance.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or de‑identify it.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Manage Subscriptions: Apple Subscriptions.
  • Access, Update, Delete: You may request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing minhhai12anqb@gmail.com.
  • Opt‑out of Marketing: We currently do not send marketing emails. If that changes, you can opt out anytime.

EEA/UK Residents: Where GDPR applies, our legal bases include performance of a contract (providing the app), legitimate interests (improving and securing the service), and compliance with legal obligations.

Children

Daily-Lan is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to protect your information as described in this Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last Updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

Contact

If you have any questions or requests, contact minhhai12anqb@gmail.com.
